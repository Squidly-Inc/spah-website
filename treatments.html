Treatments & Crafts

Choose from our pampering spa treatments and creative craft activities to build the perfect party experience.

Feel-good pampering that leaves every guest calm, glowing, and refreshed — right at home.

Manicure

Our nail techs clean, buff, file, and polish nails using disposable tools. Guests choose polish from our trendy colors as well as a special topcoat. Glitter, splatter paint, and sprinkles are popular topcoats. The party celebrant always gets extra attention. Our nail stations consist of a small table and chairs that we bring to the event.

Pedicure

SPAH pedicures also feature exciting polish colors and topcoats. We do not use any metal tools. Our nail techs clean, buff, and polish the nails. Our pedicure station is similar to our manicure station; we bring a small table and two chairs. Guests enjoy sitting in our relaxation area while their polish is drying.

Mini Facials

SPAH mini-facials are performed on a massage table. The guest's face is cleaned and cooling cucumber slices cover their eyes, while a mask is applied. Hands and heads are massaged while the mask refreshes their face. Next, the mask is gently removed with a towel and their skin is finished with a light moisturizer.

Hair Styles

SPAH hairstylists create works of art! Each guest receives a unique style that compliments their hair. Guests can request a certain style, or ask for a surprise for a custom look. Some styles, especially braids, can stay in place for a few days, making braids a good choice for sleepovers.

Massage

Our massage therapists offer relaxing chair or table massage. The length of the massage depends on the number of guests but is usually around ten minutes each. It's just enough time to feel the benefit of the massage, without missing out on the party.

Makeup

SPAH makeup artists apply light makeup to highlight our guests' natural beauty and add a touch of sparkle.

Between treatments, guests create inspired spa products to take home. SPAH party coordinators bring a craft table, all of the required supplies, a tablecloth, and bags to wrap up the guests' creations.

Lip Balm

Lip balm has been one of our most popular crafts over the last ten years. Guests decorate the containers, using gems and duct tape to personalize the outside, before blending colors and flavor oils to make their own product. The containers can also be decorated to work with different party themes.

Moisturizing Body Spray

After decorating the spray bottle, guests smell and blend different scents to create their signature scent. Add white or rose gold shimmer to make the spray sparkle.

Bath Salts

Jars are personalized, then guests enjoy smelling different essential and fragrance oils before blending them to create a unique scent. Next, they add the scent and color to Epsom/sea salts. Mixing in some Castile soap makes the salts bubble in the tub. Layering the salts in a jar creates a sand-art effect.

Sugar Body Scrub

Guests decorate their containers with gems and duct tape. Next, they combine dry and wet ingredients to make a scrub. Adding scent and color makes each scrub unique.

Hair Detangler

Making hair detangler using moisturizing ingredients, water, and scent, is both fun and practical. We like to think of detangler as a perfume for the hair. Decorating the bottle makes them unique.
